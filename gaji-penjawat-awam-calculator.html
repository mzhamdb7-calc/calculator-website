    Input details

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to estimate Malaysian public-sector salary based on basic pay and common allowance/deduction inputs.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter basic salary, allowances, and deductions. Review the estimated gross and net salary output.

    Formula used

    Estimated net salary = basic salary + allowances − deductions.

    Example calculation

    If salary plus allowances is RM 4,000 and deductions are RM 500, estimated net salary is RM 3,500.
