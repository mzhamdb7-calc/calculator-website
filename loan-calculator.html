    Mortgage / Personal Loan Calculator

    Estimate your monthly payment, full loan interest, and full payment value.

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to estimate monthly payment, total interest, and total repayment for a loan.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter loan amount, annual interest rate, and loan term in months.

    Formula used

    Monthly payment = P × r × (1+r)^n ÷ ((1+r)^n − 1), where r is monthly interest rate and n is number of payments.

    Example calculation

    A RM 100,000 loan at 5% yearly for 120 months gives an estimated monthly instalment using the amortization formula.
