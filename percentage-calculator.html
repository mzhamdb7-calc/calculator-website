    Percentage Calculator

    Calculate percentage values quickly.

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to find a percentage of a number, such as discounts, marks, commissions, and simple ratios.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter the percentage and the number. The result updates automatically when enough information is entered.

    Formula used

    Result = percentage ÷ 100 × number.

    Example calculation

    20% of 150 = 30.

    References

    Percentage meaning

    A percentage means a value expressed out of 100.
