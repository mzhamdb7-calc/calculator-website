    Credit Card Interest Calculator

    Estimate card interest using balance, APR and days.

    Input details
    Result previewEnter values to see the calculation here.

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to estimate credit-card interest for a balance over a selected number of days.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter card balance, APR, and the number of days interest is charged.

    Formula used

    Estimated interest = balance × APR ÷ 100 × days ÷ 365.

    Example calculation

    RM 2,000 at 18% APR for 30 days gives about RM 29.59 interest.

    Credit card interest

    Check your card statement or issuer terms for the exact interest method, grace period, and fees.
