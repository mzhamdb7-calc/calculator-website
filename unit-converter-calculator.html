    Unit Converter Calculator

    Choose a unit type and convert between units.

    Examples: length km to mile, weight kg to lb, volume liter to gallon, temperature c to f.

    Input details

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to convert common units such as length, weight, temperature, area, volume, and speed.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Choose the unit type, enter the value, choose the starting unit and target unit, then read the converted result.

    Formula used

    The calculator uses fixed conversion factors between supported units. Temperature conversions also include an offset.

    Example calculation

    1 kilometre = 1000 metres. 1 inch = 2.54 centimetres.
