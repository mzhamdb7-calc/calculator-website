    Rental Yield Calculator

    Estimate gross and net yield from rent and property cost.

    Input details

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to estimate rental yield from property price and rent.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter property price, monthly rent, and optional annual costs if available.

    Formula used

    Gross rental yield = yearly rent ÷ property price × 100. Net yield also subtracts supported costs.

    Example calculation

    RM 2,000 monthly rent is RM 24,000 yearly. If property price is RM 500,000, gross yield is 4.8%.

    References

    Rental yield

    Real returns may include vacancy, maintenance, assessment, management fee, tax, insurance, and financing cost.
