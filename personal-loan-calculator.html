    Personal Loan Calculator

    Estimate your personal loan monthly payment, total interest, and total payment value.

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to estimate personal loan monthly instalment, total interest, and total repayment.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter loan amount, annual interest rate, and loan term in months.

    Formula used

    Monthly payment = P × r × (1+r)^n ÷ ((1+r)^n − 1), where r is monthly interest rate and n is number of payments.

    Example calculation

    A RM 10,000 loan at 5% yearly for 60 months gives an estimated monthly payment using the amortization formula.
