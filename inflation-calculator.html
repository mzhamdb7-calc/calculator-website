    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter today’s amount, yearly inflation rate, and number of years.

    Formula used

    Future value = amount × (1 + inflation rate)^years.

    Example calculation

    RM 10,000 at 3% inflation for 10 years becomes about RM 13,439 in future cost.
