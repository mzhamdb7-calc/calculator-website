    Loan Comparison Calculator

    Compare Loan A and Loan B side by side.

    Input details

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to compare two loan options side by side and see which one may cost less overall.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter the shared loan amount, then enter rate and term details for option A and option B.

    Formula used

    Each loan option uses the amortized payment formula, then compares monthly payment and total interest.

    Example calculation

    Compare a 5-year loan at 6% with a 7-year loan at 5.5% to see the payment and interest difference.

    References

    Loan comparison

    Loan cost depends on rate, term, principal, fees, and payment schedule.

    General note

    Always compare effective interest rate, fees, penalties, insurance, and total repayment from the lender.
