    Compound Interest Calculator

    Calculate future value, total interest, and the compound interest formula.

    A = P(1 + r/n)nt
    Compound Interest = A − P

    Tip: choose monthly if the interest is added every month.

    Instructions

    • What does this calculator do?
      It calculates how much your money can grow using compound interest.
    • How to use it
      Enter principal, annual rate, years, and compounding frequency. Then press calculate.
    • Formula used
      A = P(1 + r/n)nt. Compound Interest = A − P.
    • Example calculation
      P = 1000, r = 5%, t = 10 years, n = monthly. Future value is about 1647.01.
