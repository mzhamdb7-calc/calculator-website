    Compound Interest Calculator

    Calculate future value, compound interest, and principal amount.

    Tip: choose monthly if the interest is added every month.

    What does this calculator do?

    This calculator helps you estimate how much your money can grow over time when interest is added repeatedly.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter the principal amount, annual interest rate, time in years, and compounding frequency. Then press calculate compound interest.

    Formula used

    A = P(1 + r/n)nt
    Compound Interest = A − P

    Example calculation

    If P = 1000, r = 5%, t = 10 years, and n = monthly, the future value is about 1,647.01 and the compound interest is about 647.01.
