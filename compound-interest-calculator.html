    Compound Interest Calculator

    Calculate future value, compound interest, and principal amount.

    Tip: choose monthly if the interest is added every month.

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to estimate how money grows when interest is added repeatedly over time.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter principal, annual rate, time, and compounding frequency. Then calculate the future value and interest earned.

    Formula used

    A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt). Compound interest = A − P.

    Example calculation

    P = 1000, r = 5%, t = 10 years, n = monthly gives a future value of about 1647.01.
