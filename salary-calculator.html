    Salary Calculator

    Estimate monthly and yearly take-home salary.

    Input details

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to estimate gross salary, deductions, net monthly salary, and estimated yearly take-home income.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter gross salary and optional deductions such as EPF, SOCSO, tax, or other monthly deductions.

    Formula used

    Net salary = gross salary − total deductions.

    Example calculation

    If gross salary is RM 5,000 and deductions are RM 800, estimated net salary is RM 4,200.

    References

    Salary estimate

    Use your payslip, employment contract, and official deduction rates for accurate salary information.

    General note

    This calculator is an estimate only and may not include every allowance, deduction, tax rule, or employer policy.
