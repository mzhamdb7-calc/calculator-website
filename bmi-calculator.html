    BMI Calculator

    Check your Body Mass Index.

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to estimate Body Mass Index and waist-to-height ratio from your height, weight, and optional body details.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Choose SI or US units, enter weight and height, then optionally add waist, age, gender, activity level, and target weight.

    Formula used

    SI BMI = weight kg ÷ height m². US BMI = weight lb ÷ height in² × 703. Waist-to-height ratio = waist ÷ height.

    Example calculation

    70 kg and 170 cm gives a BMI of about 24.2.
