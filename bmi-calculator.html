    BMI Calculator

    Check your Body Mass Index.

    Details
    Option▾

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to estimate Body Mass Index, waist-to-height ratio, activity-based calorie guidance, and optional target details.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Choose SI or US units, enter the Details fields, then open Option for name, age, and target weight details.

    Formula used

    SI BMI = weight kg ÷ height m². US BMI = weight lb ÷ height in² × 703. Waist-to-height ratio = waist ÷ height.

    Example calculation

    70 kg and 170 cm gives a BMI of about 24.2.
