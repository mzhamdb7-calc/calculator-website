    Basic Calculator

    A clean everyday calculator for addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, percentage, brackets, square root, and powers.

    Previous calculation

    Tip: you can also type with your keyboard. Press Enter to calculate, Backspace to delete, and Esc to clear.

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this for quick everyday maths such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, powers, and square root.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Type or tap the numbers, choose the operator, then press =. Use AC to clear and ← to delete one character.

    Formula used

    It follows the normal order of operations: brackets and powers first, then multiplication/division, then addition/subtraction.

    Example calculation

    8 + 2 × 3 = 14 because multiplication is calculated before addition.

    FAQs

    Can I use brackets? Yes. Example: (8 + 2) × 3.

    Can I use percentage? Yes. Type 50% or tap %. The calculator treats it as 50 ÷ 100.

    Can I reuse the last answer? Yes. Tap ANS after making one calculation.
