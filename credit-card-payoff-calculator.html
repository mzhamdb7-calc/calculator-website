    Credit Card Payoff Calculator

    See how long it takes to clear a card balance.

    Input details
    Result previewEnter values to see the calculation here.

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to estimate how long it may take to pay off a credit-card balance.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter card balance, APR, and planned monthly payment.

    Formula used

    The calculator applies monthly interest from APR, subtracts your payment, and estimates the months to payoff.

    Example calculation

    A RM 3,000 balance at 18% APR with RM 300 monthly payment estimates payoff time and interest.

    References

    Credit card payoff

    APR, balance, fees, and payment amount affect credit-card payoff time.

    General note

    Actual card interest can differ because of billing cycles, late fees, cash advances, and minimum payment rules.
