    Age Calculator

    Calculate your normal age and Asian age instantly.

    Name

    Date details

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to calculate age from a birth date, including years, months, days, birthday countdown, and related age details.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter the birth date. Use the calculation date to calculate age on a specific day instead of today.

    Formula used

    Age is calculated by comparing the birth date with the target date and counting completed years, months, and days.

    Example calculation

    A person born on 15 January 2000 can see exact age, next birthday countdown, and days lived.
