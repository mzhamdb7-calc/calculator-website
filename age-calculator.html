    Age Calculator

    Calculate your normal age and Asian age instantly.

    Name

    Date details

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to calculate age from a birth date, including years, months, days, birthday countdown, and related age details.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter the birth date. Use the calculation date to calculate age on a specific day instead of today.

    Formula used

    Age is calculated by comparing the birth date with the target date and counting completed years, months, and days.

    Example calculation

    A person born on 15 January 2000 can see exact age, next birthday countdown, and days lived.

    FAQs

    Does the Age Calculator calculate automatically?
    Yes. The result updates automatically after you enter or change the birth date, calculation date, or name. There is no Calculate button.

    Why is the calculation date filled in automatically?
    It defaults to today so the calculator can show your current age immediately after you choose a birth date.

    Can I calculate age on a past or future date?
    Yes. Change the calculation date to see the age on that specific date.

    What is Asian age?
    Asian age is shown as the target year minus birth year plus one, which is commonly used in some age-counting systems.
