    How to use it

    Enter distance, fuel efficiency, fuel price, and optional people sharing.

    Formula used

    Fuel needed = distance ÷ 100 × L/100km. Cost = fuel needed × price.

    Example calculation

    250 km at 7 L/100km uses 17.5 L of fuel.
