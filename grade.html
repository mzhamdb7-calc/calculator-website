    Pointer Grade Calculator

    Calculate GPA/CGPA using credit hours and grade points. Add each subject, enter its credit hour and pointer, then the result updates automatically.

    Subject Credit hour Pointer

    How to use

    1. Enter each subject or course name.
    2. Enter the credit hour for that subject.
    3. Enter the pointer/grade point, such as 4.00, 3.67, 3.33, 3.00, and so on.
    4. The calculator multiplies credit hour × pointer, then divides total quality points by total credit hours.

    Formula

    GPA / pointer = total quality points ÷ total credit hours

    Example: If one subject has 3 credit hours and 4.00 pointer, its quality point is 12.00.
