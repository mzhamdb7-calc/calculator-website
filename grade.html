    Grade Calculator

    Enter your score and total marks to calculate percentage and letter grade.

    How to use

    1. Enter the score obtained.
    2. Enter the total marks.
    3. The calculator shows percentage, letter grade, and status.

    Default grade scale

    A: 80–100, B: 70–79, C: 60–69, D: 50–59, E: 40–49, F: below 40.

    This is a general grade estimate. Your school, college, or exam board may use a different scale.
