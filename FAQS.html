    FAQs

    General questions

    Are these calculators free?

    Yes. The calculators are free to use directly in your browser.

    Are the results exact?

    The results are estimates based on the inputs you provide. Financial and health results should be used as a guide only.

    Do I need to press a calculate button?

    Most calculator pages update automatically after you enter the required inputs. The basic calculator keeps its keypad style.

    Can I copy, save, or share results?

    Supported calculators include result actions such as copy, save, share, or report links depending on the page.

    Is my input saved online?

    No server database is connected in this static website version. Some browser features may use local storage only on your device.
