    Tax Calculator

    Estimate tax using your entered income, relief and tax rate.

    This page uses the tax rate you enter manually.

    Input details

    What does this calculator do?

    Use this to estimate tax from income and deduction inputs.

    Instructions

    How to use it

    Enter annual income and any supported deduction or relief fields. Review the estimated taxable amount and tax output.

    Formula used

    Estimated tax is calculated from taxable income after supported deductions, using the calculator’s included rate assumptions.

    Example calculation

    If income is RM 60,000 and deductions are RM 10,000, the calculator estimates tax from RM 50,000 taxable income.
